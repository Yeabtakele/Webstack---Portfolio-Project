Yirgacheffe

Yirgacheffe coffee refers to high-quality Arabica coffee beans grown in the Yirgacheffe region of southern Ethiopia. This region is known for producing some of the world’s best coffee, with a reputation for being spicy, fragrant, and sweet. Yirgacheffe coffee is particularly renowned for its: Fruit-forward flavor: Yirgacheffe coffee is known for its bright acidity and flavors of citrus, floral, and fruit notes, such as orange, lemon, and bergamot. High-quality certifications: Many Yirgacheffe coffees are available as Fair Trade Organic certified, ensuring sustainable and responsible farming practices.Terroir: The region’s tropical climate, tropical forests, and unique soil composition all contribute to the coffee’s flavor profile
